Amur leopard escapes exhibit at South Carolina zoo

📅 2026-07-24 13:00 🔗 阅读原文 🔙 返回日历 📡 同步中...

July 23 (UPI) -- A South Carolina zoo revealed the animal whose escape prompted the facility to remain closed Thursday was an Amur leopard.

7 月 23 日（UPI）——南卡罗来纳州一家动物园透露，周四因逃跑而导致该设施继续关闭的动物是一只阿穆尔豹。

The Greenville Zoo said on social media that Irina, a 4-year-old Amur leopard, left her enclosure Thursday morning and was loose within the zoo grounds.

格林维尔动物园在社交媒体上表示，一只 4 岁的阿穆尔豹伊琳娜 (Irina) 于周四早上离开围栏，在动物园内自由活动。

The leopard was safely tranquilized about 9:20 a.m. and returned to her enclosure.

上午 9 点 20 分左右，这只豹子被安全地注射了麻醉剂，然后返回了围栏。

The zoo said it would remain closed for the rest of the day Thursday while officials investigate the escape.

动物园表示，周四剩余时间将继续关闭，官员将对这起越狱事件进行调查。

The City of Greenville earlier confirmed the Greenville Zoo's opening would be delayed Thursday morning and Cleveland Park was closed to the public while police and zookeepers attended to the situation.

格林维尔市早些时候证实，格林维尔动物园的开放时间将在周四上午推迟，克利夫兰公园对公众关闭，警察和动物园管理员正在处理情况。

"Cleveland Park and the Greenville Zoo are temporarily closed while zoo personnel and first responders contain an animal that left its exhibit. The animal is inside the zoo walls. Greenville Police and Firefighters are on scene to keep the public at a safe distance," the zoo said in a statement provided to WYFF-TV.

动物园在向 WYFF-TV 提供的一份声明中表示：“克利夫兰公园和格林维尔动物园暂时关闭，动物园工作人员和急救人员控制了一只离开展览的动物。该动物在动物园围墙内。格林维尔警察和消防员在现场，与公众保持安全距离。”

Cleveland Park has since reopened.

克利夫兰公园此后重新开放。

A parent whose child attends day camp at the zoo received a message Thursday morning reading: "Hello Jr. Zoologist families. We have an emergency at the Greenville Zoo. Children that have been dropped off already are in the Brook's playground. Please stay away from the zoo. We will update you as soon as possible."

一位孩子在动物园参加日间营的家长周四早上收到一条消息，上面写着：“小动物学家家庭大家好。我们格林维尔动物园遇到紧急情况。被送走的孩子已经在布鲁克的游乐场了。请远离动物园。我们会尽快通知您。”
