Ukrainian drones hit more sites of Russian online retailer Wildberries

📅 2026-07-22 22:37 🔗 阅读原文 🔙 返回日历 📡 同步中...

Ukrainian drones have hit warehouses belonging to Russia's biggest online retailer again, injuring 15 people, according to the company's founder and regional governors.

据该公司创始人和地区州长称，乌克兰无人机再次袭击了俄罗斯最大在线零售商的仓库，造成 15 人受伤。

Logistics hubs run by Wildberries in two south-west cities were impacted by overnight attacks and workers evacuated, the founder said.

该创始人表示，Wildberry 在西南两个城市运营的物流中心受到夜间袭击的影响，工人被疏散。

It is the second time Ukraine has attacked the firm's facilities in four days, with warehouses in Tambov and Elektrostal hit over the weekend, killing eight people.

这是乌克兰四天内第二次袭击该公司的设施，周末坦波夫和埃莱克特罗斯塔尔的仓库遭到袭击，造成八人死亡。

Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky said on Wednesday that Ukraine had hit logistics hubs in the Krasnodar and Stavropol regions "involved in providing the Russian army with drone components, navigation equipment and other gear".

乌克兰总统泽连斯基周三表示，乌克兰袭击了克拉斯诺达尔和斯塔夫罗波尔地区的物流中心，“涉及向俄罗斯军队提供无人机部件、导航设备和其他装备”。

Workers were evacuated after the attacks on logistics centres in Krasnodar and Nevinnomyssk in the Stavropol region, Wildberries founder Tatyana Kim said.

Wildberry 创始人塔季扬娜·金 (Tatyana Kim) 表示，斯塔夫罗波尔地区克拉斯诺达尔和涅温诺梅斯克的物流中心遭到袭击后，工人们被疏散。

"There are no words to describe all the feelings and emotions caused by the attacks on ordinary people, on our employees, on us, who are simply doing our jobs," she wrote on Telegram. "But these situations demonstrate the most important thing: we must continue to do what we do for the benefit of people."

她在 Telegram 上写道：“无法用言语来形容对普通人、对我们的员工、对我们这些只是在做我们工作的人的攻击所造成的所有感受和情绪。” “但这些情况表明了最重要的事情：我们必须继续为人民的利益做我们所做的事情。”

Five people were hurt in a warehouse complex on the outskirts of Nevinnomyssk, Governor Vladimir Vladimirov reported, with everyone receiving outpatient treatment.

据州长弗拉基米尔·弗拉基米罗夫报道，涅温诺梅斯克郊区的一个仓库大楼内有五人受伤，所有人都在接受门诊治疗。

In Krasnodar, ten people were injured in an attack on a warehouse complex, with three in a serious condition, the region's governor Veniamin Kondratyev said.

克拉斯诺达尔地区州长韦尼亚明·康德拉季耶夫 (Veniamin Kondratyev) 表示，克拉斯诺达尔的一座仓库综合体遭到袭击，造成 10 人受伤，其中 3 人伤势严重。

BBC Verify has analysed footage and confirmed fires in the two locations.

BBC verify 分析了镜头并确认了这两个地点的火灾。

Reports of the strikes first emerged just after 00:00 GMT, with the Russian state news agency TASS reporting that air defences had been activated in Nevinnomyssk. Dramatic footage filmed by locals began to emerge shortly after.

有关袭击的报道首次于格林尼治标准时间 00:00 后出现，俄罗斯国家通讯社塔斯社报道说，涅温诺梅斯克的防空系统已启动。不久之后，当地人拍摄的戏剧性片段开始出现。

In both locations, the impact appears substantial, with large fires engulfing the buildings and huge columns of smoke rising into the air.

在这两个地点，影响似乎都很严重，大火吞没了建筑物，巨大的烟柱升到空中。

Krasnodar's governor said the region was hit by a "massive attack last night", with drone debris also hitting the grounds of a company in Armavir and killing one employee. The city administration reported that an oil depot was struck, causing a fire.

克拉斯诺达尔州长表示，该地区遭受了“昨晚的大规模袭击”，无人机碎片还击中了阿尔马维尔一家公司的场地，造成一名员工死亡。市政府报告称，一座油库遭到袭击，引发火灾。

Drone fragments also hit two apartment buildings in Krasnodar and two homes in the village of Dinskaya, Kondratyev said. There were no injuries.

康德拉季耶夫说，无人机碎片还击中了克拉斯诺达尔的两栋公寓楼和丁斯卡亚村的两栋房屋。没有人受伤。

Zelensky said Ukraine had struck an oil depot, as well as a tanker and four cargo ships of the Russian shadow fleet in the waters of the Sea of Azov and Black Sea.

泽连斯基表示，乌克兰在亚速海和黑海海域袭击了一个油库以及俄罗斯影子舰队的一艘油轮和四艘货船。

Russia has been operating a "shadow fleet" to evade international sanctions imposed on its oil exports after its full-scale invasion of Ukraine in 2022.

俄罗斯在2022年全面入侵乌克兰后，一直在运营一支“影子舰队”，以逃避对其石油出口实施的国际制裁。

The overnight strikes mark the second targeting of Wildberries' facilities in less than a week.

此次连夜袭击标志着 Wildberry 设施在不到一周内第二次成为攻击目标。

Recently published satellite imagery gives an indication of the damage caused to other Wildberries sites that were hit by Ukrainian strikes over the weekend.

最近发布的卫星图像显示了周末乌克兰袭击对其他野莓地点造成的损害。

In Elektrostal, a city near the capital Moscow, three buildings were completely gutted.

在首都莫斯科附近的埃莱克特罗斯塔尔市，三座建筑物被完全摧毁。

Serhii Kuzan, chairman of the Ukrainian Security and Cooperation Centre, previously told the BBC that Wildberries' website had been used by Russian volunteers to buy military equipment.

乌克兰安全与合作中心主席谢尔希·库赞(Serhii Kuzan)此前告诉BBC，俄罗斯志愿者曾利用Wildberry 的网站购买军事装备。

"The primary rationale for striking Wildberries' warehouses is to disrupt Russian logistics and the supply of dual-use goods, critical electronics, sanctioned goods and the like to the Russian army and Russian arms manufacturers," he said.

他说：“袭击 Wildberry 仓库的主要理由是扰乱俄罗斯的物流以及向俄罗斯军队和俄罗斯武器制造商供应军民两用商品、关键电子产品、受制裁商品等。”

"The collateral damage from such strikes could also have a serious impact on the Russian economy, as well as a psychological effect on Russian society and, likely, on continued support for the war."

“此类袭击造成的附带损害还可能对俄罗斯经济产生严重影响，并对俄罗斯社会产生心理影响，并可能对战争的持续支持产生影响。”

Unlike retail giant Amazon, Wildberries and its rival Ozon act almost entirely as marketplaces, connecting independent sellers with buyers across Russia, storing and delivering their goods, and handling payments.

与零售巨头亚马逊不同，Wildberry 及其竞争对手 Ozon 几乎完全充当市场，将独立卖家与俄罗斯各地的买家联系起来，存储和交付货物，并处理付款。

The recent strikes have impacted hundreds of sellers whose livelihoods depend on online marketplaces.

最近的罢工影响了数百名依赖在线市场谋生的卖家。

Trader Elena, not her real name, told BBC Russian she had lost clothing she sells in the attacks, which is her only income.

交易员埃琳娜（化名）告诉 BBC 俄语频道，她丢失了在袭击中出售的衣服，这是她唯一的收入。

She said that "if there are more strikes, I probably won't manage. It's terrifying to keep loading my goods into what feels like a furnace".

她说，“如果罢工再多，我可能就受不了了。继续把我的货物装进一个像熔炉的地方，这太可怕了”。
