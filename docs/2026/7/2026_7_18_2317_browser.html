Husband's research pays off with $500,000 lottery win

📅 2026-07-18 23:38 🔗 阅读原文 🔙 返回日历 📡 同步中...

July 17 (UPI) -- A Maryland woman credited her husband's research with helping them to win $500,000 from a Maryland Lottery scratch-off ticket.

7 月 17 日 (UPI)——一名马里兰州妇女称赞她丈夫的研究帮助她们赢得了马里兰州彩票刮刮乐彩票 50 万美元。

The Dundalk woman told Maryland Lottery officials she and her husband often play the lottery together.

这位邓多克女士告诉马里兰州彩票官员，她和她的丈夫经常一起玩彩票。

"We play because we love it," she said. "It's something we can have fun with together. We once won $500. That was exciting."

“我们玩是因为我们喜欢它，”她说。 “这是我们可以一起享受的事情。我们曾经赢过 500 美元。那太令人兴奋了。”

The woman said she was heading to the store in late June when her husband asked her to pick up a specific scratch-off game.

这位女士说，六月底她正要去商店，这时她的丈夫让她买一款特定的刮刮游戏。

"I'd been checking the remaining big prizes on the lottery website and Gifts of Green had a few top prizes still available," the husband said. "So that was the one."

“我一直在彩票网站上查看剩余的大奖，绿色礼物还有一些头奖，”丈夫说。 “原来是这个。”

The woman picked up two of the tickets, and she and her husband scratched them off together at home.

女子捡到了两张票，她和丈夫一起在家里刮掉了。

"My eyes went back and forth between the [dollar sign] symbol and the $500,000 beneath it and the game's rules," the wife said. "I could not believe what I was seeing."

“我的目​​光在[美元符号]符号和其下方的 500,000 美元以及游戏规则之间来回移动，”妻子说。 “我简直不敢相信我所看到的。”

Her husband noticed something was unusual.

她的丈夫注意到有些异常。

"When I looked in her eyes and heard the tremor in her voice, I knew it was something big," he said.

“当我看着她的眼睛并听到她声音中的颤抖时，我知道这是一件大事，”他说。

The couple said their $500,000 prize will go toward a new vehicle and saving for their impending retirement.

这对夫妇表示，他们的 50 万美元奖金将用于购买一辆新车，并为即将到来的退休储蓄。

"We've been trying to figuring out how retirement was going to work for us. This takes away so many of the worries," the wife said.

“我们一直在努力弄清楚退休后如何为我们服务。这消除了很多担忧，”妻子说。
