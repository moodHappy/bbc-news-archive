Bear takes a dip in Colorado woman's backyard kiddie pool

📅 2026-07-19 06:09 🔗 阅读原文 🔙 返回日历 📡 同步中...

July 17 (UPI) -- A Colorado black bear was caught on camera beating the summer heat by taking a dip in a resident's backyard kiddie pool.

7 月 17 日（合众国际社）——摄像机拍到一只科罗拉多黑熊在居民后院的儿童泳池里畅游以抵御夏日的炎热。

Katie Cole shared video from the bear's visit to the pool she had filled with water on the deck in her backyard in the Steamboat Springs area.

凯蒂·科尔分享了这只熊参观她在斯廷博特斯普林斯地区后院甲板上装满水的游泳池的视频。

Cole said the pool is there for use by her dogs, but she was happy the bear was able to use it to cool off from the summer heat.

科尔说，这个游泳池是供她的狗使用的，但她很高兴这只熊能够用它来避暑。

Colorado Parks and Wildlife warned early in the summer that bear sightings in the state were likely to increase due to drought conditions. CPW recorded 98 bear sightings as of late April, compared to a total 127 statewide for all of 2025.

科罗拉多公园和野生动物部门在夏初警告说，由于干旱，该州的熊目击事件可能会增加。截至 4 月底，CPW 记录了 98 起熊目击事件，而 2025 年全州总共有 127 起熊目击事件。
